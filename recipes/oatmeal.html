Oatmeal

Description

Here is an easy filling recipe to fuel your busy mornings.

Ingredients

  • Quick Cooking Oats (1/2 Cup)
  • Nuts of Choice (1 oz)
  • Frozen Blueberries (1/2 Cup)

Steps

  1. Step 1: Pour a cup of water to a small pot and bring to a slight boil before adding in your oats. Allow this to cook for 10-15 minutes or until desired consistency.
  2. Step 2: Next, place your oats in a heatproof bowl and top with blueberries and nuts.
  3. Step 3: Enjoy!
